Minestrone

Return to homepage


Minestrone is an amazing, vegetable-forward soup for fall.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb spicy italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1/2 pound green beans cut into even sized pieces
  • 8 oz spinach, roughly chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 jar of your favorite pasta sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp dried italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can red kidney beans
  • 1 can great northern beans

Directions

  1. Heat oil in stockpot over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, celery, and italian seasoning, Saute until onion is translucent and soft, 2-3 minutes. Add sausage and garlic and continue cooking until sausage is browned all over.
  2. Add all other ingredients except for spinach, stir to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, until green beans are cooked through. Adjust seasoning as desired.
  3. Add spinach and stir until spinach is wilted; serve immediately.
